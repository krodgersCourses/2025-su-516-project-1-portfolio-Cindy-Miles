My Family

My husband of 36 years, Russel, and I live on the former dairy farm near Weippe that his family has owned since 1949. My husband's family homesteaded on the Weippe Prairie, so our children are fifth generation "Weippers." We have 2 sons and 3 daughters, ages 12-32. We also have a 2-year-old grandson and a 1-year-old granddaughter. All 4 of our adult children live in Lewiston, Idaho, which is only 70 miles from where we live, so we get to see them often.
